Getting to Know Us

Welcome to The Way Church, we are a group of believers in Christ that prioritize loving one another as God's family, promoting honesty in our Christian walk.

What We Believe

Here at The Way Church, we believe in the core values of the bible and what the church stands for. Truth is over tradition, which also means God's Word over tradition.

Scripture teaches us that Jesus' Way, and not only leading us to salvation, but also the Way to a fulfilling Christian life.

Our Mission

Our mission here at The Way is to shepherd life of every believer onto the Way of God's order & design.

Our Vision

Our vision as a church is to make disciples as God calls us to do and plant more churches. We want to shepherd every believer of Christ onto the Way of God's order & His design.

Our Values

  • To love as God loved his one and only Son.
  • To light the way and the truth for all believers and non-believers.
  • To show integrity.
  • Jesus' Way is the only way and faith through Him brings us salvation.
