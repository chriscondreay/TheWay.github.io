    What Is Wednesday Night Church?

    Wednesday Night marks the beginning of The Way Church's story. It is here where we continue to gather together as a family to have open conversations about the bible and ways we can change each of our lives with Christ by our side.

    Where

    We get together in the comfort of one's home located on the West side of Simi Valley. Upon joining, you will receive the home address every week leading up to this event.

    When

    Every Wednesday Night! Unless cancellation or update has been announced, we come together at 7pm.

    Why

    There is nothing like feeling God's love with the love and support of our brothers and sisters. We warmly welcome all who are interested and invite you to contact us directly if you have any questions.
